Vibe: Hypnotic, organic, premium. The spiral draws the eye inward. Works beautifully animated (slow spin on app splash). Most artistic of the set.
C

Orbit Ring

A garment hanger orbited by a motion ring — tech meets laundry, delivery in motion

Green
Dark
Vibe: "Your clothes are in orbit." Combines the laundry signal (hanger) with the tech/motion feel. The orbiting dot could represent the rider in transit — nice double meaning for an ops-heavy delivery service.
D

Ripple P

Letter P made of concentric ripples — freshness, water, impact

Green
Dark
White
Vibe: Impact ripple — like a drop hitting water. The "P" becomes the brand monogram inside the ripple. Simplest to reproduce and the strongest at tiny sizes because the P is always readable. If you want something that works on a 16px favicon, this is it.
E

Yin-Yang Cycle

Two interlocking swooshes — dirty→clean, pickup→delivery, balance

Green
Dark
Vibe: Pickup → Clean → Deliver → Repeat. The two arrows represent Presso's circular service model. The opacity difference (bold top = clean, faded bottom = in-process) adds depth. Best option if your brand story centers on the full-cycle journey of each order.
